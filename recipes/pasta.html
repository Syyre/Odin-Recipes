Pasta dish

The following recipe is designed to create a perfect pasta dish to your liking.

Ingrediants:

  • Bolognese Pasta
  • Tumeric
  • Coriander
  • Chicken breast
  • Protein Powder
  • Chicken stock

Method:

  1. Boil Pasta evenly
  2. Cut chicken breast into equal pieces
  3. Mix together with chicken stock
  4. Add 3 scoops of protein powder for maximum gainz
  5. Add coriander
